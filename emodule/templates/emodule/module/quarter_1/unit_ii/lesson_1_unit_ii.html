Different Kinds of Basketry Products

  1. COILED BASKETRY is a type of technique in basket making, which Native Americans of the Southwest are all renowned for. This comes in various styles and shapes depending on the materials used and the type of stitch used to interlock the coil together

    COILED BASKETRY

  2. PLAITING BASKETRY is similar to textile weaving where the material is plaited or woven to produce a wide variety of items like bags, baskets, purse, fan, etc.

    PLAITING BASKETRY

  3. TWINNING BASKETRY uses materials from roots and tree bark. Twinning actually refers to a weaving technique where two or more flexible weaving elements or weavers cross each other as they weave through the stiffer radial spokes.

    TWINNING BASKETRY

  4. WICKER AND SPLINT BASKETRY uses materials like reed, cane willow, oak, and ash to create useful basket designs.

    WICKER AND SPLINT BASKETRY
