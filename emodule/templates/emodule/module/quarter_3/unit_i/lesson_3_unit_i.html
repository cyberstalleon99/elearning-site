Production in Process of Basketry Products

Production is defined as the process of creating goods and services. It involves processing and assembling materials using simple tools, machinery, and equipment. Production requires a set of raw materials to be able to produce a set of products.

Our country is blessed with raw materials that can be used in the production of macramé and basketry products. The raw materials commonly used in making macramé products include abaca twine, plastic twine, and rattan while basketry products use raw materials such as abaca, tikug, pandan, buri, and other materials found in the locality.

Popular macramé products include belts, bags, plant hangers, and accessories. Popular basketry products, on the other hand, include market bags, school bags, and food trays.
