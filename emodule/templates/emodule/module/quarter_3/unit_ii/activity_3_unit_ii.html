Activity

  1. Form a group with four to five members and visit a basket weaving industry. Ask permission from the owner or manager to allow your group to observe how the production operation is being done. Take note of the following:
    1. The production process. Based on what you have learned, critique the sequence of their production operation.
    2. Propose a production plan for the operation. Explain the innovation or improvement you have made.
  2. Visit http://www.sustainablewinegrowing.org/docs/cswa_may07_swc_article.pdf (Retrieved October 2, 2015). Draw inspiration from this case study when planning to produce products learned from the lesson. Get ready to share in class what you have learned.

Activity 2

  1. In your neighborhood, visit two handicraft stores. Request the owner to show you his/her methods in computing the cost and selling price of his/her product.
  2. List down three reasons why you should use a scoring rubric in evaluating products and performance.
